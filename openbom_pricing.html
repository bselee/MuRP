Pricing and Subscription FAQs

What licensing and subscription OpenBOM provides

OpenBOM provides tier based subscription. There are four tiers – Free, Team, Company, and Enterprise.

Free gives you an instant 14-days trial. Also, we provide free non-commercial subscription available to qualified educational institutions, non-profit engineering teams and other organizations. Check with us about qualification for non-commercial subscription.

Team subscription focuses on making engineering data available for business (Item, BOM, Inventory, RFQ, purchasing). 

Company subscription focuses on providing a Collaborative Workspace for product life cycles and change management. It includes functions to manage CAD files versions, vault, check-out/in, complex xBOM management, Item/BOM revisions, ECO process and additional security capabilities like multi-factor authentication.

Enterprise subscription focused on a custom enterprise integration including special REST API use cases, integrations with ERP, CRM, PLM and also hosting OpenBOM platform in your AWS account.

OpenBOM provides seat based licenses. Each user must have a dedicated seat license to use a product. The price is per seat. All seats have the same price (special price for read-only seats). If you have an old grandfathered subscription please contact OpenBOM Support for transition to seat based licenses.

If you have questions about subscriptions, your historical plans and pricing, please contact support@openbom.com.

How is OpenBOM subscription work?

OpenBOM provides monthly or yearly subscriptions. The subscriptions can be purchased online using this page. Please use your OpenBOM Account email or follow the instructions on the purchasing page. If you have any questions, please contact support (support@openbom.com). For more information about OpenBOM subscriptions, please read OpenBOM Terms of Services online.

What is implementation consulting and how it works?

OpenBOM provides a consulting service that helps you learn about OpenBOM’s capabilities and align your requirements with the potential implementation scope. This service is offered during free trials and includes dedicated consulting support and meetings over a one-month period to ensure that OpenBOM meets your requirements.

What payment options are available?

You can pay for your subscription using credit cards online (via the BlueSnap credit card payment system) or by invoice and bank transfer to OpenBOM’s Bank of America account in the United States.

What is the difference between data storage and file storage

OpenBOM Is a data management system. The data you store in OpeBOM – items, properties, records, BOMs, etc. However, you can attach files and upload files to OpenBOM (using specific property type – reference properties).

Is there a limit how much data I can store in OpenBOM?

OpenBOM doesn’t limit you on how much data you can store in OpenBOM.

What is included in onboarding service?

OpenBOM provides a special premium onboarding experience. Check details in the OpenBOM Training Library – https://help.openbom.com/onboarding-experience/

What is the cost of onboarding service?

OpenBOM Onboarding training is included in annual subscription. If you want to purchase Onboarding Training with monthly subscription, the price is $1,500.

Is there a free plan for Education and non-commercial usage?

OpenBOM provides a free subscription plan for education projects and personal users such as a hobby or home projects. Check the Pricing –> Non-commercial menu on the website. The plan is for a single user only. You cannot use it together with free Design subscription. Contact support@openbom.com to ask about your project or organization qualification for non-commercial subscription.

What are the differences between OpenBOM plans?

OpenBOM features are different between plans. Check the feature table above to see what features are supported. The transitions between plans are automatic, your data is preserved, no migration is needed. For more details about OpenBOM features, check help.openbom.com.

Where can I find detailed information about OpenBOM product features and functions?

Please navigate to OpenBOM Online Training (help.openbom.com) for detailed product documentation, videos, training course, webinars, and product F.A.Q.

Can I use the same user account multiple times in different browser tabs (or windows)?

Absolutely, you can open as many windows and tabs as you want in the same browser

Can I use the same user account in CAD integration and browser at the same time?

Absolutely, you can use OpenBOM in CAD (Eg. Onshape, Autodesk Fusion, Dassault Systemes SolidWorks, etc) and use it in the browser at the same time.

What if I forgot to sign-out from my computer in my office/ home and I need to sign-in on another device?

OpenBOM’s alert message will give you an option to sign out remotely.

Will the OpenBOM session expire automatically?

Yes, the OpenBOM session expires automatically in 24 hours after you sign in. So, you will need to re-login after 24 hours. However, to release the license you need to sign-out from the service.

Are there any differences between different users in my team with licenses (Eg. read-only user vs Editing user)?

Administrator can configure your team account with a different access right using administrator dashboard (edit, user-defined view, read-only, special access) . OpenBOM provide special discounts for View only licenses.

Can I use the same user account multiple times on different computers?

No, you can use an OpenBOM user in a specific device and browser. If you want to use it on another device, you need to sign out from your other active session.

How to purchase an additional seat?

You can click on “Buy” command and specify the number of additional seats you want to purchase. Contact sales@openbom.com if you have questions.

Is there a cost for OpenBOM CAD Add-ins

Some CAD integrations are included in OpenBOM team and company subscriptions. For a detailed list of what is included in a specific subscription, please check our integration page (openbom.com/integrations)

How to install OpenBOM CAD Add-ins?

Installation packages are available for each integration on OpenBOM Integration Page (openbom.com/integrations).

Should I pay separately for CAD Add-ins?

It depends on the CAD Add-in. Please check OpenBOM Integration Page (openbom.com/integrations).

What’s the difference between Team and Company subscriptions?

Team and Company subscriptions are different in features. Navigate to the list of detailed features to learn more.

Is there a limit to the number of BOMs I can create in the free or paid plans?

No limits for the number of BOMs, Catalogs, Vendors, etc. OpenBOM Storage size is not limited.

How can a Team (or Company) administrator add more users to OpenBOM?

OpenBOM Team/Company administrator has access to a special menu in the OpenBOM tool that allows managing users – add, remove, and manage their access rights.

Is there an annual subscription plan, i.e. I pay a full 12-month subscription up front?

Yes, we provide monthly and annual (yearly) plans. The annual plan is for 12 months paid upfront.

Can I cancel a subscription plan at any time?

Yes, you can. However, keep in mind we have a “no refund policy”. A canceled paid monthly plan will conclude at the end of the monthly term. A canceled annual (yearly) plan will conclude at the of the annual term. We will respect the privacy of your data per our Privacy Policy. Monthly and Annual subscription plans renew automatically unless canceled per the Terms of Service.

How to delete my free account?

To delete your account, please contact support@openbom.com

What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

Your data is private and confidential to you and all registered users regardless of subscription license. If you cancel the subscription, your account automatically will turn into a free trial with expired trial. Should you choose to delete your account we will not retain your data. More info can be found at Privacy Policy.

What is the Enterprise subscription?

The Enterprise subscription applies to organizations that wish to deploy OpenBOM with additional security options, develop custom integrations, or support custom or private hosting options. Check features in the table above. Please email support@openbom.com for more information.

Does OpenBOM support Multi-Factor Authentication?

OpenBOM supports MFA at the Company Subscription level.

I did't find the answer on my question. What should I do?

Please contact support @ openbom dot com.
